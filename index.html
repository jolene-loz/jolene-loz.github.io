About Us

Who We Are

The Bostonians of Boston College is BC's original and premiere a cappella group. For THIRTY crazy years we've been making music, traveling from coast to coast, and having a whole lot of fun along the way. Our classic co-ed sound has been heard from Mexico to Montreal and on over ten studio recorded albums and EPs.

Tour

This year is going to be a blast. We've got awesome gigs and trips lined up that will be sure to blow you away. We've traveled to California, Montreal, Virginia, Washington DC, New York City, Disneyland, Michigan, Notre Dame, New Jersey, Cape Cod, the Hamptons, Quebec, Ireland and all over New England!

We LOVE music

We recorded our ninth and tenth studio albums. Our most recent album, "Flipping Out," was nominated for Best Mixed Collegiate Album in the national CARA awards (the Grammys of a cappella), as was our 2nd most recent album, "Brandy Station". Look out for our new album coming out soon!
